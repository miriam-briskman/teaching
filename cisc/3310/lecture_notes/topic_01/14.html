Hardware Components of a Computer

  1. ROM (Read-Only Memory) is a small chip that stores a short program called BIOS that runs when the computer “boots” (turns on or restarts): it preps the computer hardware and operating system for the computer to flawlessly launch and work. ROM is non-volatile, and cannot be deleted since it is "read-only" (deleting the contents of the ROM will result in the computer not being able to launch properly.)
