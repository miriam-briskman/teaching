Hardware Components of a Computer

Hardware components that every computer has are:

  1. The motherboard is a large board-like chip hosting multiple devices and chips that are essential for the computer's operation, such as the CPU, RAM (memory), cache, graphics cards (GPU), buses and wires connection all these devices, and many more. Every modern desktop and laptop computer, every cell phone device, and many other devices have a motherboard.
