Intro to Computer Architecture: Motivation

The previous example emphasizes that the functionalities of a car matter the most: they can tell how useful this car will be to you and, most likely, how many years it will serve you. The same idea applies to any electronic device or product, including computers and smart devices.

In this course, we will learn how computers are built and how they work.
In other words, this course will focus on the hardware aspects of a computer and the way in which all hardware components interact with each other to create this nowadays-essential but, nevertheless, marvelous device. This knowledge will not only make you a better programmer but a mindful computer user and buyer.
