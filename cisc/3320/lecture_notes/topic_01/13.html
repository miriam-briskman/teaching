Operating System Brand Examples

Laptops/Desktops:

  • Windows
  • Mac OS
  • Chrome OS
  • Linux/UNIX

Phones/Tablets/Smart Watches:

  • Android
  • iOS

Every electronic device or appliance contains an operating system, no matter how simple its functionality is.
