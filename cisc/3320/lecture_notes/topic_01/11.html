Software Types

Examples of programs included in application software:

  • Office productivity apps: Microsoft Office, LibreOffice, Google's G Suite, etc.
  • Gaming apps: Minecraft, Gold Miner, Bertie the Brain, etc.
  • Music apps: Spotify, Pandora, YouTube Music, etc.
  • Video conference apps: Zoom, Skype, Class Collaborate, Microsoft Teams, etc.
  • Emails transfer apps: Gmail, Yahoo! Mail, Thunderbird, Microsoft Outlook, etc.
