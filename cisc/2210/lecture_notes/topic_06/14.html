Exercises

Fun Class Activities:

  1. Arrange the letters A, B, C in all possible orders (e.g., ABC, ACB, BAC, etc.) How many ways are there?
  2. A race with $5$ runners: how many ways can they finish first, second, and third?
  3. How many ways can $6$ people sit in a row?
  4. Choose $4$ cards from a deck of $52$ cards. How many ways?
  5. Pick $3$ toppings for a pizza from $10$ available toppings. How many ways?
  6. How many distinct permutations of BANANA are there?
  7. How many distinct ways to rearrange SUCCESS are there?
  8. Arrange PEEP in distinct orders.
